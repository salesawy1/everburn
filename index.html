RESTORING YOUR PURCHASE

If you unlocked the full game and later reinstall or switch devices, open the paywall and tap “Restore purchase.” Your unlock is tied to your Apple ID — one purchase, forever, no re-buying.

PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: July 2026.

Everburn does not collect any data. The game runs entirely on your device. We have no servers, no analytics, no accounts, and no third-party trackers. Your save file and your progress never leave your phone.

The only network interaction is Apple’s own In-App Purchase system, which processes the optional one-time unlock. That transaction is handled entirely by Apple under Apple’s Privacy Policy — we never see your payment details, and we receive no personal information from it.

Because we collect nothing, there is nothing for us to share, sell, or lose. If this ever changes, this page will be updated before any such feature ships.
